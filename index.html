About

On September 2020, Lyricsz page on facebook was first created and was proposed to start function around May, 2021. Till date with an exception of not focusing actively on Facebook.

Lyricsz was created by a team of enthusiastic. With a simple question in mind, why must it be difficult to find a song lyrics online? Or to figured out if it was correct or not? Or why should someone try to answer this question before having the confident that he/she now knew the song? All this are the motives for the creation of Lyricsz back then, and it quickly grew out of hands.

It was first renamed Spinditty, named after a band... The creator of Lyricsz owns a band named SPINDITTY. Apparently, Lyricsz still stood for a very long time and we have no intention of changing the name, whether now or in the future.

The main purpose for the creation of Lyricsz has been diversify in it's early years, now the team are focsuing more on features like Lyrics Video creation, Game creation, A site dedicated to artist all over the world to collaborate, App creation and lots more. This has nothing to do with the main vision and the creator has no guarantee of any kind that the current goal won't be diversified in the future.
